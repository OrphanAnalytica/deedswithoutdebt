Title Research
Verified property ownership through county records
Checked for superior liens (IRS, mortgage)
Confirmed no bankruptcy filings by owner
Verified no pending foreclosure actions
Checked for judgments against property
⚠️ Warning:
IRS liens and mortgage liens are almost always superior to tax liens. You could pay for the lien and still lose the property!
